Voucher sale paused

The number of people holding Air Experience Flight vouchers in expectation of flights has grown significantly, mainly due to adverse flight conditions over the summer.

Canberra Gliding Club has decided to halt the sale of any more vouchers until we can clear the backlog and provide new flights in a reasonable period of time.

We're sorry about this because we enjoy flying with new people and aim to help you enjoy it as well.

Normal service will be resumed as soon as possible.
